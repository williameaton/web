About me

I am a graduate student in the Theoretical and Computational seismology at Princeton University. My research interests are in geophysical numerical modelling, predominantly in seismology and geodynamics.

Outside of academia my heart lies in exploring nature, Liverpool FC and drinking tea.

This website is currently rather empty but I aim to continue adding to it through my PhD. You can find more details on my research interests and projects below. Feel free to contact me for a chat about anything geophysical!
